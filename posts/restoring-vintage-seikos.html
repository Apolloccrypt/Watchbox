Restoring Vintage Seikos

A guide to breathing new life into classic Seiko timepieces.

Step 1: Check with seller if the watch runs

Usually, this is already mentioned in the description.

Step 2: Checking the value online for a fair bid

Using Google Lens + the caseback number, you can quickly find pricing. Keep in mind that vintage pieces vary due to the state of the watch, completion of the set (box/papers), and rarity.

Step 3: Receiving the watch

Check if the watch runs by carefully shaking it if it's automatic or winding if it's a handwinder. Also check the time and date settings.
